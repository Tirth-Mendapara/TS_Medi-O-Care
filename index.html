About Us

Who we are and our mission

TS Medi-O-Care™ — We care for the mediocres

Founded by Shriyan Tandale and co‑founded by Tirth Mendapara, TS Medi-O-Care™ is an emergency response platform designed to save lives through technology and community. Our mission is to provide instant access to emergency services, blood donor networks, and AI‑powered first aid guidance — all while keeping user safety and privacy at the forefront.

We believe that every second counts in an emergency. That's why we built a system that connects you to nearby hospitals and offers reliable first‑aid advice when you need it most.

Our vision: A world where no one faces a medical crisis alone. We care for the mediocres — because everyone deserves a lifeline.

👨‍💻 Shriyan Tandale – Founder & Creator

Shriyan Tandale is the visionary behind TS Medi-O-Care™. With a passion for leveraging technology to save lives, he designed the core emergency response system, integrated real-time location sharing, and built the blood donor network. His mission is to make emergency assistance accessible to everyone, especially those who often get overlooked – the "mediocres".

Expertise: Full‑stack development, emergency response systems, AI integration.

👨‍💻 Tirth Mendapara – Co‑founder & Creator

Tirth Mendapara co‑founded TS Medi-O-Care™ to bring a collaborative vision to life. He focuses on user experience, community outreach, and ensuring that the platform remains intuitive and reliable in high‑pressure situations. Tirth believes that technology should serve humanity, and he works tirelessly to keep the platform free, fast, and friendly.

Expertise: UI/UX design, community management, emergency protocols.
